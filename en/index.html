01 - About

My background

Currently in my 3rd year of a Bachelor in Computer Science, Game Development at Gaming Campus (Lyon), specialising in gameplay mechanics and original system design.

I prefer games that teach you their rules without explaining them; that’s the philosophy that has guided the way I design my mechanics from the very beginning.

Having grown up with a Wii in the living room, then discovered Warcraft III and Minecraft on my first PC, I quickly realized that what drew me in wasn’t just playing, but understanding why these experiences worked, and wanting to recreate them. A naturally logical mindset and a growing curiosity about programming languages and their internal mechanisms did the rest.
